My Teaching Method

I first work to identify each student’s unique learning style, then determine which part of their foundation in the subject (s) is missing. This allows me to present the information and process in a manner that the student can relate to. In depth knowledge of the subjects allows me to focus on finding and presenting the material in a manner that is both effective and efficient. I know that students require patience and perseverance from their tutor and I have tutored students of all ages and levels to understand that I live for the moments when students finally grasp a concept or problem. My method ensures that we are always moving forward towards the student’s and parent’s ultimate goals. I encourage feedback and involvement from parents to ensure everyone is comfortable with the progression.
